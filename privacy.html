Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

FleetSignal respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and stored when using FleetSignal.

Information We Collect

FleetSignal may collect the following information:

  • Email address used for account login
  • Vehicle registration details entered by users
  • Optional vehicle information such as insurance dates or vehicle names
  • Premium subscription status
  • Reminder preferences and alert settings

How Your Information Is Used

Your information is used to:

  • Provide MOT, tax, and insurance reminder services
  • Store vehicle information securely
  • Manage premium subscriptions
  • Improve FleetSignal features and reliability
  • Send important service-related emails

Email Reminders

FleetSignal may send reminder emails relating to MOT, tax, or insurance dates if alerts are enabled on your account.

Users remain fully responsible for ensuring their vehicles remain legally compliant at all times. Reminder delivery cannot be guaranteed.

Payments

Premium payments are processed securely through third-party payment providers such as Stripe. FleetSignal does not store full payment card information.

Data Storage

Vehicle and account data may be securely stored using third-party cloud providers including Supabase and Vercel. Reasonable measures are taken to protect user information.

Third-Party Services

FleetSignal may use third-party services including:

  • Supabase
  • Stripe
  • Vercel
  • Resend
  • DVLA APIs

Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting FleetSignal support.

Changes To This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of FleetSignal constitutes acceptance of any updated policy.
